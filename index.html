Scuba Diving

What does SCUBA even stand for?

Self-contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus

    Equipment you will need:
  1. Tank; full of air
  2. Buoyancy Control Device (BCD)
  3. A regulator, like an Octopus Reg or an Air2 that comes with your BCD
  4. Snorkel
  5. Defog
  6. fins and booties
  7. weight, if local diving
  8. Dive Computer

    9. Things you want before and after:
      it is always good to eat a little before you dive so you dont get sick. always have water on hand for after when you are thirsty because of all the salt. Have a couple snacks in your car or on the boat so you can stay healthy and keep up your strength.
